VORTIGATE

Founded in 2023 and based in Amman, Jordan, Vortigate is a pioneering technology company specializing in immersive solutions. We are committed to transforming businesses through cutting-edge technologies such as Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR). Our innovative services cater to industries across Saudi Arabia, Dubai, and Jordan, helping businesses deliver unparalleled customer experiences and operational efficiency.

Strategic Direction

Mission

A Brief History Of

Our Journey

Vortigate was established in 2023 in Amman, Jordan, by a team of passionate innovators who recognized the untapped potential of virtual reality (VR) in reshaping industries. The founders envisioned a company that could harness the power of immersive technologies to drive innovation, improve education, and transform businesses across the region.
